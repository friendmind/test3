I suppose I can give you an update

A couple things have happened since the last time we've spoken. The main one being I got a hair cut. That might not be big news or anything but to me it is. As you know, I had wanted to cut my hair. Actually, I don't even know if that is what I told you. I think I just said I didn't trust my mom cutting my hair anymore. Anyways, I had told my sister, Edith, and our friend, Susan, if they would be down to go to the nail salon to get my hair cut. This nail salon is where they both get there nails done and where they happen to cut hair as well. Since it was a place that was familiar, I considered going there to get a hair cut. I pitched we should go there soon but we never set a date to go. Fast-forward a week later, my sister shows up unexpectedly and tells me if I am ready to go to the nail salon. I was caught off guard as by that time I had decided to grow out my hair. As you could imagine, I was struggling to make a decision. It was now or never my sister said. After a minute of deep thinking, I said fuck it and off we went. My older sister, Mariana, ended up tagging along as well. So we had the whole gang there: both of my sisters, Susan (our friend) and Nadia (Susan's daughter).

I was nervous, of course, as I had not gotten my hair cut by someone other than my mom or away from home before. I was scared that they would mess up the hair cut. But you know what, good or bad hair cut, I was going for the experience. If I didn't like the cut I'd just grow it out, no big deal right? Well, I took a seat on one of the fancy chairs they had and was met with a huge mirror in front of me. I'm glad I don't have perfect vision because seeing them cut my hair with perfect vision would have only made me even more nervous.

I was fine for the first half of the hair cut. It wasn't until she kept changing guards that I began to get nervous again. At some point, she took out an electric razor that had a sharp blade and let me tell you she was going at it with it. She was doing things I have never experienced before when getting a hair cut so I was panicking a bit. But I told myself to trust the process. That she knows what she's doing. Fast-forward to the end of the hair cut, I'm glad I trusted the process because I loved how it turned out. It did feel weird seeing myself with short hair again as I had gotten accustomed to the weight and look longer hair had given me. But for the first time, I actually got the hair cut I asked for 😂 My friend was recording the experience so when I got up there she was with the camcorder getting the full look reveal. Everyone had a positive reaction to my hair cut which I was not expecting at all. Even the other worker there complemented me. She said it looked good and that short hair suited me. I was flattered by her last comment as no one has ever told me that before. As you can expect, I will definitely be returning there if I want a new look.

As for the look, I really do look and feel different. Crazy what a hair cut can do to a person. Anyways, the only person who was not happy with the look was my mom, which was expected. She has always preferred my hair long. But I did not mind her reaction. I am happy with my hair cut and that is what matters.
